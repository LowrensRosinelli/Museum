Exhibitions & Events

Explore the many wonders of science at our museum. From the vastness of space to the smallest microbes on Earth, there’s something for everyone!

Exhibitions

Cosmology

Explore the night sky, discover galaxies, and learn about the history of navigation by the stars.

Evolution

Learn about Darwin’s theory of evolution, meet extinct creatures, and discover the origins of life on Earth.

Biology & Medicine

Take a look at the breakthroughs in medicine and biology that have changed our lives for the better.

Robotics & AI

Witness the information revolution in action: from industrial robots to AI in our daily lives.

Ecology

Understand the impact of human activity on the planet and explore sustainable solutions for our future.

Special Events

Visiting Professor of Aeronautics

Join us for a series of lectures by Prof. Sheila Widnall from MIT, exploring the past and future of aeronautics.

Night in the Museum

Bring your family for an overnight adventure among dinosaurs and science displays. Don’t forget your sleeping bag!

Energetica Exhibition on Loan

Experience the power of the elements with this interactive exhibit from the NEMO Science Museum in Amsterdam.
