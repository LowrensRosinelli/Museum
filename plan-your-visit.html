Plan Your Visit

Find all the practical information you need for an enjoyable day at the Community Science Museum.

Location & Admission

The museum is located at Kjelsåsveien 143, 0491 Oslo.

Entrance is free for all. Guided tours leave every hour and cost 70 NOK per person.

If you have a group of 6 or more, please contact us to arrange a private guided tour.

Hours

  • Monday: Closed
  • Tuesday: 10:00 – 16:00
  • Wednesday: 10:00 – 16:00
  • Thursday: 10:00 – 16:00
  • Friday: 10:00 – 19:00
  • Saturday: 9:00 – 16:00
  • Sunday: 9:00 – 13:00

Accessibility

We have wheelchair ramps, audio guides, and Braille display signs available for visually impaired visitors.

Food & Drink

Our café offers light lunches, snacks, and refreshments to keep you energized.

Shop

Stop by our gift shop for fun science memorabilia and activity packs for all ages.
